Character Best Weapons and Artifacts

Navia

Navia is a geo DPS that focuses on using Elemental Skill as a source of damage. Every time Navia gets a shield from the Crystalize reaction, she will get a bullet stack that increases the damage of Elemental Skill.

Navia can work well as a quickswap DPS that is only on-field to unleash her Elemental Burst and Elemental Skill, similar to Tighnari, or as a Main DPS wherein she stays on-field to deal powerful Geo Damage from her Elemental Skills and the geo infusion it gives to Navia's Normal Attack.

Base Atk 674

CRIT Rate 22.1%

Many Oaths of Dawn and Dusk: Increases ATK by 20%. When characters in your party obtain Elemental Shards from Crystallize reactions, the equipping character will gain 1 Seal, increasing Elemental Skill DMG by 18%. The Seal lasts for 15s, and the equipped character may have up to 2 Seals at once. All Seals disappear 0.2s after the Elemental Skill deals DMG.

  • Nighttime Whispers In The Echoing Woods

    Best in Slot Tailor made for her. Navia increases damage when she absorbs Crystalize. This artifact works well with Navia and is best for boosting damage. If you are still farming for the perfect set of Echoing Woods, you can opt to use ATK set and Geo Set.

  • Golden Troupe

    When using Golden Troupe, opt for a quickswap playstyle. DMG Bonus is very important for Navia since she has many sources of ATK with passive and outside buffs, making Golden Troupe an amazing set. However, when using Golden Troupe, make sure to opt for a quickswap to retain the DMG boost from the 4-piece set.
